South Hall Dining — Yelp and Reddit compilation

South Hall is known for international food nights on Thursdays. The Indian food station is legit — several students from the South Asian student association confirmed the spices are authentic, not cafeteria bland. Lines on Thursday dinner can exceed 35 minutes.

Lunch at South is faster than North according to multiple posts, averaging 10-15 minutes except on Thursday. The stir fry station lets you pick your own ingredients which is popular with athletes after practice.

Downsides: South Hall's building is older and the AC breaks every September for about a week. Mold was reported in a storage closet in 2024 and housing did an inspection, but the dining area itself was cleared. Several 1-star reviews mention fruit flies near the compost bins in summer.

The late-night snack window (9pm-midnight) only runs Sunday through Wednesday. A lot of students wish it was every night.
