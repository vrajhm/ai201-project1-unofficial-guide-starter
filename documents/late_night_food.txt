Late Night Food Options — student survival thread

After midnight your options are slim. South Hall late night (Sun-Wed 9pm-12am) serves fries, pizza, and cereal. North Hall closes at 8pm sharp — no exceptions.

The 24-hour diner on College Ave accepts dining dollars until 2am on weekends only. It's a 15-minute walk from central campus and students say the mozzarella sticks are "grease therapy during finals."

Food trucks on Library Lawn Friday nights take cash and card, not meal swipes. The taco truck is the crowd favorite; the burger truck has inconsistent hours.

Many off-campus students recommend keeping $50 dining dollars specifically for late-night West Village coffee during exam weeks when South late night isn't running.
