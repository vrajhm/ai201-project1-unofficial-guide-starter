Top Freshman Dining Mistakes — orientation mentor handout

Mistake 1: Using all 19 swipes in the first two weeks. Pace yourself — you can't bank them.

Mistake 2: Assuming the housing lottery changes where you can eat. It doesn't. Pick your dorm for location, not dining access.

Mistake 3: Skipping breakfast swipes. That's when North and South are empty and food is freshest.

Mistake 4: Eating at South on Thursday without a buffer in your schedule. International night lines are legendary.

Mistake 5: Trusting the dining app menu without walking over first. Always verify if you have allergies or dietary restrictions.

Mistake 6: Not knowing West Village takes dining dollars for most vendors. Save dollars for when main halls are closed.

Mentor recommendation: Find one "home base" dining hall and learn its off-peak hours in your first month. Most upperclassmen swear by this.
