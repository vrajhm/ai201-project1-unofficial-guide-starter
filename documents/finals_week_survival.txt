Finals Week Dining Survival — anonymous student blog repost

North Hall extends hours to midnight during finals week only. South does not extend hours — check the official calendar because this changes every semester.

Caffeine: West Village coffee shop opens at 6am during finals. Lines start at 5:45. The dining halls have mediocre drip coffee; bring your own mug for a 10-cent discount that nobody remembers to ask for.

Stress eating consensus from the thread: South Thursday international night during finals is a mistake — too crowded. North's pasta station at 3pm is the sweet spot.

Free fruit baskets appear near the North exit during finals — rumor is the dean's office sponsors them but nobody confirmed. They run out in under an hour.

If your meal swipes are running low, the food pantry in the student union accepts dining dollar donations and gives emergency meal vouchers — no questions asked according to three student org leaders.
