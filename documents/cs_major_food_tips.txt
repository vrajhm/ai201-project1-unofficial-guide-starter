CS Major Food Tips — department Discord pinned message

If you have back-to-back classes 12-4, pre-grab food at North before 11:30 or you'll miss the window entirely. The CS building has no real food, only vending machines that eat your money.

Professor Patel (CS 201) famously tells students to "eat before my Friday exams" because the exam room is far from any dining hall and he doesn't allow food in the room.

Study groups often meet at West Village because of outlets and caffeine. The unofficial rule: claim a table by buying one coffee, not by leaving a backpack — staff will clear unattended bags.

Hackathon weekends: South Hall opens early at 6am for sponsored events only. Regular students can't get in without an event wristband. Plan ahead or order delivery.

Best value: North Hall breakfast swipe uses half the mental energy of deciding lunch. Several TAs said they survived grad school on 9am omelets.
