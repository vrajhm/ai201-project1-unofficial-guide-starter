Campus Sustainability Club — dining audit notes

Composting bins exist at North and South but contamination is high. The club's 2025 audit found 40% of compost bins had plastic wrappers. Dining services is piloting clearer signage in spring 2026.

Reusable container program: you pay a $5 deposit for a green box, return it to any dining hall for a clean one. Participation is low because the return bins are hidden — North's is behind the dish return, South's is near the loading dock.

Local sourcing claims on the website are partially true. The salad greens come from a farm 30 miles away April through October. Winter produce is standard Sysco delivery despite marketing language.

Water refill stations were added at all halls in 2023. Plastic bottle sales dropped 18% according to the sustainability office annual report.
