Unofficial Meal Plan Guide — orientation wiki paste

The default freshman meal plan is 19 meals/week + $200 dining dollars. Upperclassmen overwhelmingly switch to the 14-meal plan and use dining dollars at West Village.

Meal swipes do NOT roll over week to week. Dining dollars roll over fall to spring but expire at graduation. You cannot transfer swipes to another student — this is a common myth.

The housing lottery does NOT affect which dining hall you can use. Any student on a meal plan can eat at North, South, or the satellite locations. Only West Village requires dining dollars for most vendors.

To check your remaining balance: use the university app under "Campus Card" — the website portal updates 24 hours late according to multiple complaints.

Adding flex dollars mid-semester costs a 5% fee. Buying the largest block at the start of the year avoids this.
