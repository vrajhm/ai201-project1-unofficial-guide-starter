West Village Eats — Discord #food channel logs

west_village_eats: West Village is not a traditional dining hall — it's a food court with Panda Express, a salad chain, and a local coffee roaster. You use dining dollars, not meal swipes, except for the "daily special" counter.

Most CS majors camp out here between classes. WiFi is the best on campus but outlets are fought over. Lunch lines at Panda are 15-20 min; the daily special counter is usually under 5 min if you get the rice bowl.

Quality is "mall food court but slightly better." The coffee shop pastries are baked on-site and sell out by 10am. Don't bother with the sushi — multiple people got mild food poisoning in spring 2025 and it was temporarily shut down.

Hours: 7am-9pm weekdays, 10am-6pm weekends. Closed entirely during winter break unlike the main dining halls.
